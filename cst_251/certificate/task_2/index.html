Vin Diesel, (given name Mark Sinclair) is an American actor, writer, producer, and director. He is most notably famous for this role as Dominic Toretto in the Fast & Furious franchise, which will be releasing its latest, and penultimate, installment, Fast X later this year in May.

Some of the acting roles Diesel has played include:

  • Private Adrian Caparzo (Saving Private Ryan 1998)
  • The Iron Giant (The Iron Giant 1999)
  • Richard B. Riddick (Pitch Black 2000, The Chronicles of Riddick 2004, The Chronicles of Riddick: Dark Fury 2004, Riddick 2013)
  • Dominic Toretto (The Fast and the Furious 2001, The Fast and the Furious: Tokyo Drift 2006, Fast & Furious 2009, Fast Five 2011, Fast & Furious 6 2013, Furious 7 2015, The Fate of the Furious 2017, F9 2021, Fast X 2023)
  • Xander Cage (XXX 2002, XXX: Return of Xander Cage 2017)
  • Groot (Guardians of the Galaxy 2014, Guardians of the Galaxy Vol. 2 2017, Avengers: Infinity War 2018, Avengers: Endgame 2019, Thor: Love and Thunder 2022)
  • Santiago Da Costa (Ark: The Animated Series TBA, Ark 2 2022)

Diesel’s birthday is on July 18, 1967 and he was born in Alameda County, California. Early on in life, Diesel would move to New York City where he would begin acting at age 7 in a theater play, after having a run in with the theater’s owner while attempting to vandalize the place. Diesel would eventually attend Hunter College in New York, studying creative writing which led him down the avenue to gain skills as a screenwriter.
