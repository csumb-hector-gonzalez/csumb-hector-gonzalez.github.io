About San Diego!

San Diego is a coastal city in Southern California. It is known for it's beautiful beaches and many tourist spots. It's a city that sits right on the border between the United States and Mexico, with the city on the other side of it's border being Tijuana.

Being right next to the coast, San Diego has many opportunities for whale watching. The species of whales can differ throughout the year as different species have different migrating cycles.
