An Introduction To Culinary Chicken

Across the world, there are many ways to eat chicken, all of which require different methods of preperation. They each have different nutritional values and taste very different, but also very tasty! In this site, a short and brief overlook about some common ways chicken is enjoyed will be explored.

Ways Chicken is Cooked

Fried Chicken

Fried Chicken is really delicious and can be made at home or bought at:
  • Fast Food Chains
  • County Fairs
  • Theme Parks

Chicken Wings

Chicken Wings are a great bite sized meal and are often coated in various sauces. These can include:
  • Buffalo Hot Sauce
  • Teriyaki Sauce
  • Barbeque Sauce

Chicken Soup

Chicken Soup is a great way to eat chicken, but be careful it can be HOT!
To make sure you don't get burned:
  1. Wait until there's no steam rising from the bowl
  2. Check temperature by taking a little sip
  3. If not hot, enjoy!
